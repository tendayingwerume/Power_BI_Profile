FIXED ASSET PORTFOLIO


FIXED ASSET PORTFOLIO

This is a set of dashboards from the FlexyAsset Fixed Asset Management system. This is a system that I have been developing as an upgrade to its desktop predecessor. I have included 6 dashboards, each with four or more visuals. The dashboards include:

  1. Portfolio Analysis – provides a snapshot of the Asset Portfolio, including composition by Asset Group, Product Group and Operational Status.
  2. Financial Highlights – this dashboard includes Acquisition trend analysis, Depreciation and Asset Valuation as well as Age distribution by value which helps identify high value assets that are close to retirement or due for replacement.
  3. Distribution Insights – consists of visuals that show the assignment of assets, their distribution geographically as well as across the company dimension.
  4. Maintenance Status – this dashboard represents the maintenance status assets, showing the number and percentage of assets in operation, due or overdue for service, undergoing maintenance as well as those under warranty repair. gsgs
  5. Service Metrics – this dashboard takes Service Desk and Job Card data and compiles maintenance indicators such as response time, lead time, execution time, idle time, schedule variance to name a few. It measures the performance of maintenance personnel and third-party service providers.
  6. Failure Rate Analysis – this dashboard shows indicators such as Total number of failures, Failure Rate(%), Total downtime, Average Repair Time, Asset Failure Frequency by Asset Group as well as Failure Trend Analysis.

HIV Education in Schools


HIV EDUCATION IN SCHOOLS

This is a set of dashboards is based on the data format for the annual education census in Namibia. The actual data has been doctored and does not represent the actual results of the census. The aim of the census is to determine the extent to which HIV Education is taught in schools, implementation of HIV workplace programs, as well as compile statistics on learner and teacher dropout as well as learner mortality. I was initially contracted by UNESCO in 2016 and again in 2019 to develop a reporting module that would provide 7 key indicators. I have included 6 dashboards, each with four or more visuals. The dashboards include:

  1. Sexuality Education Coverage – this dashboard shows the numbers and percentage of learners from various regions who participated in Life Skills Based HIV Education in schools across Namibia, including year on year comparisons.
  2. Teacher Training – this dashboard analyses teachers who received training and then taught HIV education in their respective schools.
  3. HIV Workplace Programs – this dashboard looks at schools that implemented an HIV Workplace programs.
  4. Learner Drop Out – this dashboard analyses learners who permanently dropped out of school in the various regions.
  5. Learner Mortality – here we look at the numbers and percentages of learners who died in each school year by region and cause of death.
  6. Teacher Attrition – this dashboard looks at the number and percentage of teachers who resigned or died in each region during the school year.

Microlender Portfolio Analysis


MICROLENDER LOAN PORTFOLIO

I created this reporting tool to assist my clients who are microlenders to comply with reporting requirements of the Namibia Financial Institutions Supervisory Authority. It provides additional reports to assist managers in decision making. I have included 4 dashboards, each with four or more visuals. The dashboards include:

  1. Financial Performance – this dashboard gives insight into the number and value of loans issued, number of active clients as well as the Average Loan Value. The loans are further analyzed by Purpose, Gender and Location of clients
  2. Loan Analysis – this dashboard takes a closer look at the loans that were issued and gives a breakdown by Loan Size, Repayment Period as well as number of loans by Client Income Group.
  3. Borrower Analysis – Here we look at Client Composition by Age, Gender, Income Group as well as Frequency of Loans, any analysis that revealed that there were one-time clients as well as repeat clients at various levels up to a few that have returned for a loan every single month for two years.
  4. Application Metrics – this dashboard provides the total number of applications, number of approved and declined applications, approval and decline rates. It also includes a breakdown of rejected applications by reason for rejection as well as the number of approved and declined applications by client income group.

Retail Performance Analysis


RETAIL PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

This dataset represents a typical retail business operating in many countries across continents. I came up with a field set that represents the diversity of reports I wanted to generate. I then gave prompts to ChatGPT to generate for me a list of fake clients, store locations and transactions for three product categories, Clothing, Electronics and Furniture. I created the following 5 dashboards, each with 4 or more visuals.

  1. Financial Performance – this dashboard gives insight into the total number and value of orders received, Average Order Value, Total Profit and Profit Percentage. It also provides monthly and quarterly trend analysis for revenue and profit, as well as Year to Date Sales Growth/ Year on Year Comparison.
  2. Product Performance – this dashboard shows the Top 10 product by revenue contribution, as well as a comparison of product categories according to their revenue. It also includes a scatter plot to show price sensitivity for individual items.
  3. Customer Demographics – this dashboard shows the total number of clients who purchased at the various store locations, total sales by gender, age group as well as gender composition by product category
  4. Store Insights – this dashboard provides sales analysis by store location, top 10 stores, average order value by store, as well as monthly sales trend by location.
  5. Customer Behavior and Buying patterns – here we look at the Average Order Frequency, Average Basket Size and Average Customer Lifetime Value. Further we do a cross-category purchase analysis as well as a detailed order frequency analysis.
